Eclipse: Rise of the Vanguard

In the distant future, humanity's last great city, Aurora Prime, stands as a beacon of hope on a fractured Earth. But peace shatters when an ancient alien race awakens, led by colossal biomechanical warlords. As cities fall and skies burn, the Vanguard, an elite team of operatives with cutting-edge technology and cybernetic enhancements, steps up to defend humanity. Armed with advanced weaponry and aided by mysterious alien allies, the Vanguard must uncover the secret of the alien invaders and activate the ancient planetary defense system known as Eclipse. With time running out, the team faces impossible odds, epic battles, and a fight for the very survival of Earth.

Galactics

In a universe on the brink of collapse, an unlikely crew of diverse and heroic adventurers bands together to save their galaxy. Featuring a fearless leader, a tech-savvy alien, a cunning sharpshooter, and a reprogrammed battle droid, the team ventures into uncharted worlds teeming with danger, wonder, and humor.

Dimensions Unleashed

When a quirky yet brilliant scientist, Professor Quirk, accidentally activates his prototype "Dimension Shifter," he drags his unsuspecting intern, Milo, into a wild journey across bizarre and chaotic universes. From battling sentient UFOs to befriending alien outcasts, the duo must navigate strange new worlds to repair the device and find their way home. With each dimension more unpredictable than the last, their bond is tested as they uncover a looming threat that could collapse all realities. Can Professor Quirk's eccentric inventions and Milo's unexpected resourcefulness save the multiverse in time?
